Our Mission

The Tseaddo Business Chamber supports local business growth by providing resources, advocacy, and networking. It aims to drive economic development and foster collaboration and innovation, helping businesses thrive and enhancing the quality of life in the community.

Our Vision

The Tseaddo Business Chamber envisions a vibrant business environment driven by innovation and collaboration. It strives to be the leading resource for businesses, fostering growth, expanding job opportunities, and promoting sustainable, inclusive economic prosperity in the Accra region.

Entrepreneurship & Innovation

The Tseaddo Business Chamber focuses on empowering local entrepreneurs and fostering innovation to drive economic growth. It provides resources, mentorship, and funding opportunities to support businesses at all stages. The chamber also promotes collaboration through networking, innovation hubs, and digital transformation, helping businesses adapt and thrive in a competitive market.

Our Focus

The Tseaddo Business Chamber drives business success and community development through:

  • Business Growth & Development
  • Networking & Collaboration
  • Advocacy & Representation
  • Economic Development
  • Education & Training
